## Overview

The Hiddens is an immersive VR escape room project developed for the LED CAVE at the Leibniz Supercomputing Centre (LRZ). The experience combines environmental storytelling, horror atmosphere, and puzzle-based interaction in a stylized apartment setting. It was designed to explore how large-scale projection-based VR can intensify presence, tension, and narrative engagement.

Players take on the role of an investigator following a missing person case. After entering the apartment, they become trapped in a disturbing, crime-scene-like environment. To progress, they must explore the space, uncover clues, and solve interconnected puzzles while gradually revealing the hidden story behind the scenes.

## Key Features
Immersive VR escape room designed specifically for a projection-based CAVE environment
Narrative-driven exploration centered around a missing person investigation
Puzzle-based progression through environmental clues and hidden interactions
Stylized horror atmosphere inspired by backroom aesthetics and unsettling office-like spaces
Strong emphasis on spatial presence, tension, and environmental storytelling
